Piña Colada

| Spanish rice | Potato Salad | Cheesecake | Piña Colada | Sesame Chicken |

Ingredients

For the sugar syrup base

  • 100 g golden caster sugar
  • 100 ml water
  • 1 tsp. vanilla paste / vanilla essence
  • Zest of 1 lime

For the piña colada mocktail

  • 200 g frozen pineapple chunks
  • 150 ml pineapple juice
  • 100 ml coconut cream
  • 1 tbsp. sugar syrup
  • 1 tbsp. condensed milk
  • Maraschino cherry (optional)
  • Slice pineapple (optional)

Directions

  1. In a small saucepan add the golden caster sugar, water, vanilla. Place over low- medium heat, until the sugar has dissolved. When dissolved, turn the heat up to high and boil for 3 mins. Remove from the heat and add in the zest of lime and set aside to cool. This is your sugar syrup base and can be kept in the fridge for up to 1 month.
  2. Place all piña colada ingredients into a high-speed blender, and blend for 30 seconds until desired consistency. Pour into a glass, and garnish with a maraschino cherry and a piece of pineapple, if you wish.
